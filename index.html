Super Cactus Bros

How to play

Use WSAD or arrow keys to move, you can also use space bar to jump.

Spiders and dragons will kill you if you touch them.

Every minute a dragon will spawn. A countdown will help you to know when he is coming. To be safe from him just move somewhere else then were you have been.

Objective

To win the game you need to travel to your house wich is 5000m away.
