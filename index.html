Super Cactus Bros

How to play

Use WSAD or ←↕→ to move, you can also use Space to jump.

Spiders and dragons will kill you if you colide with them.

Every 30 seconds a dragon will spawn. A countdown will help you to know when he is coming. To be safe from him just move somewhere else then were you have been.

It is possible to walk on brick blocks. You can also use the ladders to climb.

Objective

To win the game you need to travel to your house wich is 500m away.
