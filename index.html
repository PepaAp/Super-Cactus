Super Cactus Bros

How to play

Use WSAD or ←↕→ to move, you can also use Space/W/↑ to jump. You can reset checkpoints (activated if steped on green pipes) using U while playing.

It is possible to walk on brick blocks and green pipes. You can also use the ladders to climb. Magnets will hold onto you. You can use S to let go or Space/W/↑ to jump once on the side. You can also move on magnets.

Spiders, dragons and spikes will kill you if once colide with them.

Every 10 seconds a dragon will spawn. A countdown will help you to know when he is coming. Dragons always spawn on the same height level as you so KEEP MOVING.

Objective

To win the game you need to travel to your house wich is 100m away. GOOD LUCK!
